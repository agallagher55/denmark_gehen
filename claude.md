### Companion Voucher — Full Details
**Type:** World Elite round-trip companion voucher
**Validity:** No blackout dates; must *book* before expiry, travel can occur after
**Expiry date:** September 26, 2026

**Companion base fare (international/Europe):**
| Fare class | Companion flat fee |
|------------|-------------------|
| Econo / EconoFlex | $399 CAD + taxes & ATC |
| Premium / PremiumFlex | $499 CAD + taxes & ATC |
| Business / Member Exclusive | ❌ Not eligible |

**Rules:**
- Both passengers must book the **same fare type**
- Cardholder must be travelling on the booking
- Valid on WestJet-marketed and operated flights only (no codeshares)
- Outbound from Canada: book at westjet.com
- Return leg originating outside Canada: must **call WestJet contact centre**
- Cannot be combined with discount codes
- If cancelled, voucher is reinstated with original expiry date
- UltraBasic bookings: non-refundable, voucher is forfeited if cancelled

**⚠️ Critical finding for YHZ–CPH route (transatlantic):**
- WestJet structures transatlantic fares with a **very low base fare (~$64–128/person round trip)** and **very high "Other ATC" surcharges (~$730–1,460/person)**
- The companion voucher only replaces the base fare — it **cannot touch ATC surcharges**
- On Econo fares for this route, the $399 voucher fee **exceeds** the actual base fare → costs you more money
- WestJet automatically detects this and warns you at checkout, offering to remove the voucher
- **Voucher is only beneficial on Premium/PremiumFlex** for this route, where base fare is large enough to make the $499 flat fee worthwhile
- At Econo pricing (~$441–537/person), **best to skip the voucher entirely and save it**

**Best uses for voucher:**
- Domestic Canada routes (companion flies for $119)
- Sun/Caribbean/US routes (companion flies for $119–199)
- Premium transatlantic (meaningful savings vs. two full Premium fares)
- Peak season bookings where base fares are elevated
